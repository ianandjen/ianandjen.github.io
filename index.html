Getting There

Airports

The closest airports are Westchester County Airport (HPN), LaGuardia Airport (LGA) and John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK). The Hyatt is 12 miles from HPN, 30 miles from LGA and 36 miles from JFK.

Airport Transportation

CT Limo has a shuttle service for $60/person from LGA or JFK to Greenwich. Discounts are available if you buy round trip shuttle service ($116 for one person) or travel round trip with up to 3 companions (companions pay 50% of full fare).

Car Rentals

Cars can be rented at all three airports. Take a look at http://www.kayak.com/cars or http://www.kayak.com.au/cars for rates. Parking at the Hyatt costs $10/day, and parking at the Delamar is complimentary for hotel guests.

Things to Do

The Hyatt has a comprehensive list of things to do in and around Greenwich, as does the Delamar. Here are some of our favorites.

Manhattan

Midtown is a 45-minute train ride from Greenwich. Take the Metro North train from Greenwich/Old Greenwich/Riverside to Grand Central Terminal, and you’ll have easy access to great shopping, tourist attractions, museums, and more.

  • Metropolitan Museum of Art
  • American Museum of Natural History
  • Central Park
  • Empire State Building
  • Pretzels from the street vendors (Jen’s favorite pretzels)

Shopping

Greenwich Avenue is easily accessible via the Hyatt’s hotel shuttle and is walking distance from the Delamar Hotel. “The Avenue” has lots of interesting shops and food and gets pretty busy on weekends. Farther afield are the Stamford Mall in Stamford, CT, and The Westchester, a large mall in White Plains, NY.

Other Attractions

  • Rye Playland: amusement park in Rye, NY
  • Bruce Museum: art, science, and natural history museum in downtown Greenwich
  • Maritime Aquarium: aquarium in Norwalk, CT
  • Bronx Zoo: large zoo in Bronx, NY
  • Audubon Center: nature center in northern Greenwich
  • Yankee Stadium (schedule)
  • Tod’s Point
  • Island Beach
